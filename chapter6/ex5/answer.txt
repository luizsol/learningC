Unidirectional Linked Lists: I can be used to store a sequence of items, and I make it easy to insert new items. But you can process me in only one direction.

Binary tree: Each item I store can connect to up to two other items. I am useful for storing hierarchical information.

Array: I can be used to associate two different types of data. For example, you could use
to me to associate people’s names to their phone numbers

Doubly Linked List: Each item I store connects to up to two other items. You can process me in two directions.
